FAQ:





  1. What type of wood do you recommend for the BBQ pits? We use mesquite but we recommend experimenting with different type of woods.
  1. Where should I place my BBQ pit? We recommend flat areas away from low branches and combustibles.
  1. How much charcoal or wood do I need? We recommend filling the BBQ pit with a little then adding more when the fire starts.
